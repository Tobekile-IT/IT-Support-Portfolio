Service Desk Simulation 2: Account Update – Legal
Name Change
Objective
Update a user's account following a legal name change due to marriage.
Ticket Information
Priority: Low
Request Type: Account Update
Reason: Legal name change after marriage.
Steps Performed
1. Assigned the ticket.
2. Opened the company Directory.
3. Searched for the user account.
4. Updated the legal name.
5. Updated the primary email address.
6. Added the requested email alias.
7. Verified the changes.
8. Closed the ticket successfully.
Result
Successfully updated the user's account details, primary email address and email alias.
Skills Practiced
• Ticket ownership
• User account management
• Directory administration
• Email alias configuration
• Identity management
• Documentation
What I Learned
This simulation taught me how to process account update requests accurately while maintaining
user identity records. I also learned the importance of updating email aliases so users can continue
receiving emails sent to their previous address
